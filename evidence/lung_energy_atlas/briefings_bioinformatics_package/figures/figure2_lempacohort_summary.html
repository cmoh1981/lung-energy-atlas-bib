Figure 2. LEMPA lung energy atlas case study — cohort and data layers. The LEMPA cohort comprised 130 patients with paired CT imaging and bulk RNA-seq, reduced to 123 for the H4b entropy analysis after routine missing-feature filtering. RNA-seq processing retained 148 genes overlapping the HCA reference panel and MSigDB v7.5 Hallmark collection (50 pathways). Deconvolution was performed using NNLS and scaden (600 simulated bulks, 100 steps per model, 5-fold cross-validation). Cross-method agreement at the intended cell-type surface failed (median −0.087), triggering target collapse from 50 cell types to four broad modules. HCA = Human Cell Atlas. LEMPA = Lung Energy Metabolism Pathway Atlas. NNLS = Non-Negative Least Squares.
