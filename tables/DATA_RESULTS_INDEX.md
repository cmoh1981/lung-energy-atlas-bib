## Result Interpretation Lock

The following claims are allowed:

- The atlas component is useful as a normal-lung metabolic reference.
- The paired public NSCLC radiogenomic line was executable and analyzable.
- V1 failed its prespecified cell-type predictive gate.
- V2 remained analyzable but failed deconvolution agreement and collapsed-target validation gates.
- H4b is a narrow fallback-derived association-level remainder with major caveats.

The following claims are not allowed:

- Do not claim external validation.
- Do not claim a successful radiogenomic bridge.
- Do not present H4b as rescuing H4a/H4c or the line-level result.
- Do not describe the V2 collapsed-module target as equivalent to the original cell-type target.
- Do not imply journal submission readiness until metadata, bibliography, and archive identifiers are locked.
